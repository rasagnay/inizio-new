About Resume

Should photos be included in a resume?


A resume should address your experience and skills in an articulate, concise and professional manner, so it's about knowing what to include in a resume that is of added value. For this reason, it's recommended to avoid adding a photo to your resume.

How to List Skills on a Resume?


Only list skills that are appropriate for the job you are applying for. Job ads usually list a set of requirements or skills they expect a good candidate to have. Make sure you don’t leave any of those out on your resume.

What do recruiters look for in a resume?


Ensure you include important keywords throughout your resume. This will create a link in the mind of the reader between you and the requirements of the role. Avoid excessive jargon and be mindful that the person reading it may not be a technical or industry expert; however, they will know what to look out for.
